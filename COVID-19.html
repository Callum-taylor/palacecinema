Coronavirus (COVID-19) Update


Dear Palace Cinema Guest,
                                                           
As announced by the Prime Minister, all non-essential businesses will now be forced to close. Unfortunately, this includes the Palace Cinema. Currently the Prime Minister will review the situation in three weeks. This means we only have to cancel our Easter Movie and Finding Nemo, these films were the last in our Easter schedule and we will not release any new schedules until further notice. We have also decided to pause all memberships effective 30th April 2020. This means no customers with memberships will be charged until further notice.

If you have booked our Easter Movie or Finding Nemo we will be contacting you by email with further information in the coming days.

We hope that the situation continues to improve and we can invite our guests to the Palace Cinema again in the near future. 

Warm Regards,

The Executive Team
